Khon Kaen University

มหาวิทยาลัยขอนแก่น



It is a public research university in Thailand. It was the first university established in northeastern Thailand and remains the oldest and largest university in the region.

Motto

Motto "วิทยา จริยา ปัญญา" (both Thai and Pali)
Motto in English "Knowledge, Virtues, Wisdom"
